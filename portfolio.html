Password Generator

Create a random password! This app allows you to create a ramdomized password from 8 to up to 128 characters with option to include upper, lowercase, numbers and special characters. This app uses JavaScript to respond to the user input.

Awesome Calendar

Here is an interactive calendar that helps you keep track of your to-do list. Simply type in your to-do in the input box. As the workday progresses, you can see the orange colored current hour indicator move down the list.

Should I Stay...OR Should I Go?

Are you hungry and can not decide weather to go out to a restaurant with friends and family? Or have them come over for an awesome home cooked meal? Based on the Clash song "Should I Stay or Should I Go", this app helps you make that decision by offering both recipies to try at home and a list of local restaurants. You tell the page what type of cuisine you are in the mood for and select either "Should I Stay...In?" or "Should I Go...Out".

Burger Heaven

You love burgers enough to think of them as a taste of heaven? Well, with this app you get a chance to create a holy burger and decide if it is worthy to be eaten or worthy to save and admire.

Good To Go

Due to the current pandemic of covid-19, we are encouraged to stay within our own space and keep a safe distance from one another while in public. Here is an app that allows you to search a menu and order wonderful food from a local restaurant. An optional text message will be sent notifying you that the order is ready for curbside pickup.
